# Trigonometry Content Development Notes

The course structure was developed from the open educational resource *Trigonometry, Version 2*, produced through a partnership involving Salt Lake Community College, the University of Utah, Weber State University, and Utah Valley University.

The source work states a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported license. This repository document preserves the development provenance and licensing note. Student-facing lessons are written and organized as native EduVerse content and do not expose the source document, page images, page numbers, extraction messages, or a document viewer.

Implementation principles:

- mathematical concepts were reorganized by instructional section rather than printed page;
- notation was normalized and checked against standard trigonometric conventions;
- examples were independently written or recalculated for web presentation;
- diagrams were recreated as responsive SVG components;
- exercises are presented as interactive practice with explanations;
- existing EduVerse content and routes were preserved.
