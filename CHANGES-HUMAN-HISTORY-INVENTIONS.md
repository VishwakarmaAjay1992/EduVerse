## Included

- Searchable invention directory
- Beginner, expert and researcher reading layers
- Necessity and pre-invention methods
- Global prior art and historical context
- Inventors, collaborators and enabling industries
- Documented and reconstructed experiment failures
- Engineering milestones and later improvements
- Step-by-step working principles and original diagrams
- Component/anatomy tables
- Real historical photographs, replicas and diagrams with creator/licence links
- Benefits, trade-offs, modern descendants and myth correction
- Glossary, open research questions and institutional source lists
- Header, homepage and sitemap integration
- Automated data-completeness tests

## Adding future inventions

Follow `documentation/HUMAN-HISTORY-INVENTIONS.md`. Add one typed record to `src/data/inventions.ts`; the directory, dynamic pages, homepage and sitemap update automatically.
